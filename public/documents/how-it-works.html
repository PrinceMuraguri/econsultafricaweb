⚙ The Mechanics

How Forecast Arena Works

At the heart of it: buy shares in outcomes you believe will happen.

1

Pick a Forecast Question

We publish questions about real economic outcomes — inflation, exchange rates, GDP growth, interest rates, elections, business conditions. Each is verified by official sources like KNBS, CBK, IMF, or World Bank.

2

Buy Shares in What You Believe

Each share costs between $0.05 and $0.95 — the price reflects the crowd's current belief. A $0.70 share means ≈70% probability. Each share pays $1 if correct and $0 if wrong. Forecasting without money is also free and valid.

3

Earn When You're Right

When the outcome is determined by the official Resolution Source, accurate forecasters receive payouts from the pool. Money flows from wrong to right. You can also sell your position anytime before resolution.

📈 Dynamic Pricing
